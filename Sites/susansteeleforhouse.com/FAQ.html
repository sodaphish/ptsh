Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What is the current party breakdown in the South Dakota legislature?
A: House of Representatives: 51 Republicans; 19 Democrats; Senate: 25 Republicans; 10 Democrats

Q: What is the current gender breakdown of the South Dakota Legisature?
A: House of Representatives: 56 men, 14 women; Senate: 32 men, 3 women

Q: How many lawyers currently serve in the South Dakota Legislature?
A: House: 8; Senate: 4

Q: If you are elected, will you still practice law?
A: Yes! The South Dakota Legislature meets part-time: 35 days one year and 40 the next year.
