My Family



As part of the celebration of our 35th Anniversary, we had a family photograph taken. After all, our last family photo didn't include the two newest grandchildren, Michael and James! This photo was taken in September of 2005. John and I are in the center on the top row. Our son, Corey, and our daughter-in-law, Amanda Holly, are on the right side with their three boys: John, Jonas and James. Our daughter, Amanda Christine, and our son-in-law, Christopher, are on the left side with their two children: Isabella (Bella) and Michael.





The whole family got involved in the campaign for the Dakota Wesleyan Homecoming Parade. The grandchildren wore their special sweat shirts. After the parade, my daughter and daughter-in-law insisted that we get a picture taken. I'm glad we did. You can see a lot of personality and energy in each little sweetie pie. Clockwise: Bella, Jonas, Michael, James and John.
